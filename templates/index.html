RETURN

Welcome to the world of S'nhilla, a world tarnished by evil and hatred for all things mortal. You are one of the few sentient beings left, and with vengeance in your heart, you have one goal in mind: obtain the root of all evil. Only after you achieve this will your family tree be satiated and you can claim your rightful place in this horrific world.

Select your route by clicking on the path that you would like to take. Choose wisely depending on what you need and what you can handle. From there, either collect magical artifacts, fight unspeakable horrors, or interact with the world with intelect and cunning character.

When you have defeated 5 bosses, you will have finally mapped the route of all evil. If you die though, don't worry, your family tree will be extended with your spiritual ressurection as the root, equipped with a special item you have acquired in your past live's journies.

Best of luck.
